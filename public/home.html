Welcome to Quagga Chess

What is Quagga Chess?

Quagga Chess is your chess destination where playing the game is synonymous with learning. We provide a platform that allows you to enjoy chess matches while receiving in-game tips to help you enhance your skills.

Our Mission

At Quagga Chess, our mission is clear: we're dedicated to helping people learn chess in a fun and interactive way. Through our platform, we aim to make the learning process enjoyable and accessible to everyone, regardless of their skill level.
